7.13 Crafter Kata (crafter-4)

read
Make survival game with an avatar, carrots, fish, and a crafter on tile 2 (posn 200 200) that makes both carrot-stew and fish-stew
code
#lang survival
 
(define (fish-stew)
   (custom-food
    #:name
    "Fish Stew"
    #:sprite
    fish-stew-sprite
    #:respawn?
    #f
    #:heals-by
    50))
 
(define fish-stew-recipe
   (recipe
    #:product
    (fish-stew)
    #:build-time
    40
    #:ingredients
    (list "Fish")))
 
(define (my-cauldron)
   (custom-crafter
    #:sprite
    cauldron-sprite
    #:position
    (posn 200 200)
    #:tile
    2
    #:recipe-list
    (list carrot-stew-recipe fish-stew-recipe)))
 
(survival-game
  #:avatar
  (custom-avatar)
  #:food-list
  (list
   (carrot #:amount-in-world 10)
   (fish #:amount-in-world 10))
  #:crafter-list
  (list (my-cauldron)))
