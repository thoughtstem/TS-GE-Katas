20 Homing Repeater Katas

20.1 Homing Repeater Kata (homing-repeater-1)

Tip: #:fire-mode ’homing will fire darts that follow enemies. Other options are: ’normal (default) and ’spread.
read
Make a game with a weapon that fires homing darts.
code
#lang battle-arena
 
(battle-arena-game
  #:weapon-list (list
   (custom-weapon
    #:name "Homing Repeater"
    #:sprite (make-icon "HR")
    #:fire-mode 'homing)))

20.2 Homing Repeater Kata (homing-repeater-2)

Tip: Default damage, speed, and range are 10, 10, and 1000.
read
Make a game with a weapon that fires homing darts with customized damage, speed, and range.
code
#lang battle-arena
 
(define (my-dart)
   (custom-dart #:damage 15 #:speed 8 #:range 40))
 
(battle-arena-game
  #:weapon-list (list
   (custom-weapon
    #:name "Homing Repeater"
    #:sprite (make-icon "HR")
    #:fire-mode 'homing
    #:dart (my-dart))))

20.3 Homing Repeater Kata (homing-repeater-3)

Rarity Rules:

  • ’common = 5 total

  • ’uncommon = 4 total

  • ’rare = 3 total

  • ’epic = 2 total

  • ’legendary = 1 total

read
Make a game with a ’rare weapon that fires homing darts with customized sprite, damage, speed, and range.
code
#lang battle-arena
 
(define (my-dart)
   (custom-dart
    #:sprite (rectangle 10 2 'solid 'pink)
    #:damage 15
    #:speed 8
    #:range 40))
 
(define (homing-shot)
   (custom-weapon
    #:name "Homing Repeater"
    #:sprite (make-icon "HR")
    #:fire-mode 'homing
    #:dart (my-dart)
    #:rarity 'rare))
 
(battle-arena-game
  #:weapon-list (list (homing-shot)))
