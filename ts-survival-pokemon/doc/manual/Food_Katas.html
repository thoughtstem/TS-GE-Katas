3 Food Katas

3.1 Food Kata

Tip: By default, there will be only 1 custom-food in world.

read
Make a game with a Pokemon and some custom food.
code
#lang survival-pokemon
 
(pokemon-game
  #:pokemon (custom-pokemon)
  #:food-list (list (custom-food #:amount-in-world 10)))

3.2 Food Kata

read
Make a game with a Pokemon and exactly two apples that heal you by 20.
code
#lang survival-pokemon
 
(define (my-food)
   (custom-food
    #:sprite apples-sprite
    #:name "Apples"
    #:amount-in-world 2
    #:heals-by 20))
 
(pokemon-game
  #:pokemon (custom-pokemon)
  #:food-list (list (my-food)))

3.3 Food Kata

Tip: By default, custom-food will heal by 10.

read
Make a game with a Pokemon and exactly two custom foods that heal you by 20.
code
#lang survival-pokemon
 
(define (my-food)
   (custom-food
    #:amount-in-world 2
    #:heals-by 20))
 
(pokemon-game
  #:pokemon (custom-pokemon)
  #:food-list (list (my-food)))

3.4 Food Kata

read
Make a game with a Pokemon and 2 apples (heals by 20 and respawns), and 1 cherry (heals by 50 and doesn’t respawn).
code
#lang survival-pokemon
 
(define (my-food)
   (custom-food
    #:sprite apples-sprite
    #:name "Apples"
    #:amount-in-world 2
    #:heals-by 20))
 
(define (special-food)
   (custom-food
    #:sprite cherry-sprite
    #:name "Cherry"
    #:amount-in-world 1
    #:heals-by 50
    #:respawn? #f))
 
(pokemon-game
  #:pokemon (custom-pokemon)
  #:food-list (list (my-food) (special-food)))
