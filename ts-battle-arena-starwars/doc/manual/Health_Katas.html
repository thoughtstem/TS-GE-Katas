8 Health Katas

8.1 Health Kata (health-1)

read
Make a game that has an powerup that increases your health by 50.
code
#lang battle-arena-starwars
 
(starwars-game
  #:item-list (list
   (custom-item
    #:name "Health Powerup"
    #:sprite (make-icon "HP" 'green 'white)
    #:on-use (change-health-by 50))))

8.2 Health Kata (health-2)

read
Make a game that has an powerup that recovers your health to 100.
code
#lang battle-arena-starwars
 
(starwars-game
  #:item-list (list
   (custom-item
    #:name "Max Health Powerup"
    #:sprite (make-icon "MHP" 'green 'white)
    #:on-use (set-health-to 100))))

8.3 Health Kata (health-3)

read
Make a game that has both a Health and a Max Health defined powerup.
code
#lang battle-arena-starwars
 
(define (health-powerup)
   (custom-item
    #:name "Health Powerup"
    #:sprite (make-icon "HP" 'green 'white)
    #:on-use (change-health-by 50)
    #:rarity 'uncommon
    #:respawn? #t))
 
(define (max-health-powerup)
   (custom-item
    #:name "Max Health Powerup"
    #:sprite (make-icon "MHP" 'green 'white)
    #:on-use (set-health-to 100)
    #:rarity 'epic))
 
(starwars-game
  #:item-list (list (health-powerup) (max-health-powerup)))
